About Us

HIT Seva LLC is a consulting and professional services company that provides comprehensive service solutions addressing needs in IT Reporting Services, EHR, ERP and HIM. We provide best practices to reduce costs, increase revenue realization and improve quality metrics.

Our competitive professional fee structure allows our clients to most effectively and efficiently utilize our consultants. Our team listens to your needs and works with you to define a consulting arrangement that meets your needs

Care Managers - dedicated associates responsible and accountable for the delivery of our services and solutions - act as a point of contact for partnership activities, monitor consultant performance to ensure achievement of engagement deliverables, and facilitate status reporting to our Clients
